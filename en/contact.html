Contact Us

Contact Xiake Games for bug reports, tool suggestions, privacy questions, and site feedback.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Contact email

For bug reports, tool suggestions, privacy questions, policy questions, or general feedback, email contact@xiakegames.com. Please include the page URL, device type, browser name, and a short description of the issue when possible.

What to report

You can report a game that does not start, mobile layout problems, a tool result that looks incorrect, spelling mistakes, broken links, privacy concerns, parent safety suggestions, or ideas for future tools.

How feedback is handled

Issues that affect core gameplay, tool accuracy, privacy pages, advertising review, or user safety are prioritized. General suggestions are collected for future updates.

Do not send sensitive data

Please do not send student rosters, passwords, payment data, identification documents, or unnecessary personal information in feedback messages.
