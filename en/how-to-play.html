How to play

Goal

Score points by secretly playing on your phone while the teacher is not looking. Release the button before the teacher turns around.

Modes

  • 60-second challenge: score as much as possible in one minute.
  • Survival mode: keep playing until you get caught.

Controls

Mobile players hold the phone button. Desktop players hold Space.
