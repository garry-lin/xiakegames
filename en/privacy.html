Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for Xiake Games covering local storage, Google Analytics, Google AdSense, cookies, and student safety notes.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Data we process

Xiake Games provides free games and browser tools. Game leaderboards may store nicknames, scores, and survival times in browser localStorage. Tool text is generally processed in the browser and does not require an account. We do not ask for real names, school names, classroom numbers, phone numbers, or precise location.

Google Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics to understand page views, device types, regional trends, and basic site usage. This information helps us improve content, navigation, and performance. Google may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics processing.

Google AdSense and cookies

The site has Google AdSense code installed to support free content. Google and its partners may use cookies to show, measure, and improve ads. Visitors can manage personalized ad preferences through browser settings and Google advertising controls.

Children and students

The site is designed for casual games and tools, but visitors should not share personal information. Do not use real names, school names, phone numbers, or other personal data as leaderboard nicknames, and do not paste sensitive information into tools.

Deleting data

Local leaderboard data can be removed by clearing browser site data. For privacy questions, contact us through the Contact page or email contact@xiakegames.com.
