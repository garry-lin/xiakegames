Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for Xiake Tools covering local storage, Google Analytics, Google AdSense, cookies, and visitor safety notes.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Data we process

Xiake Tools provides free games and browser tools. Game leaderboards may store nicknames, scores, and survival times in browser localStorage. Tool text is generally processed in the browser and does not require an account. We do not ask for real names, official names, group numbers, phone numbers, or precise location.

Google Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics to understand page views, device types, regional trends, and basic site usage. This information helps us improve content, navigation, and performance. Google may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics processing.

Google AdSense and cookies

The site has Google AdSense code installed to support free content. Google and its partners may use cookies to show, measure, and improve ads. Visitors can manage personalized ad preferences through browser settings and Google advertising controls.

Minor visitors and general visitors

The site is designed for general browser tools and secondary casual games, but visitors should not share personal information. Do not use real names, official names, phone numbers, or other personal data as leaderboard nicknames, and do not paste sensitive information into tools.

Deleting data

Local leaderboard data can be removed by clearing browser site data. For privacy questions, contact us through the Contact page or email contact@xiakegames.com.

Your choices

You can clear local browser data, block or delete cookies, use private browsing mode, and manage Google advertising personalization controls. Some browser settings may reduce analytics or advertising features, but core tools should remain usable.

Tool input safety

The site is designed for everyday text and calculation tasks, not for storing confidential information. Avoid entering private private records, passwords you actively use, payment data, medical records, government identifiers, or private business documents.

Third-party services

The production site uses Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These services may process technical information such as device type, approximate region, page URL, browser signals, and cookie identifiers according to Google policies.

Data minimization principle

We try to keep the site useful without collecting account profiles. Tools are designed to work without login, and game scores use local browser storage where practical. If future features require more data, the privacy policy should be updated before those features are promoted publicly.
