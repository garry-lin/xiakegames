Play Teacher Is Coming!

Use the game area below. Your local best scores stay in your browser.

Control: hold the phone button to play, release to pretend studying before the teacher looks.
If sound does not play, tap the game once first. Browsers require a user action before audio can start.

Rules

  • Score while the teacher is facing away.
  • When the warning appears, get ready to release.
  • If the teacher sees you still playing, the run ends.
  • Some warnings are fake-outs, so stay alert.

Controls

  • Mobile: hold the phone button to play; release to study.
  • Desktop: hold Space to play; release to study.
  • Enter starts a 60-second challenge.
