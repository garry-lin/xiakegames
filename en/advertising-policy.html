Advertising Policy

Advertising policy for Xiake Tools, covering ad placement, content separation, accidental clicks, and third-party ad providers.

Last updated: June 22, 2026

How ads support the site

Advertising may help keep the tools free to access. Ads should not hide the main content, block tool controls, or be presented as official site navigation.

Ad placement principles

We aim to keep advertisements away from input boxes, game controls, result buttons, and other interactive areas where accidental clicks are likely. Pages that are too thin, purely technical, error pages, or game iframe pages may be excluded from advertising.

Third-party providers

The site may use Google AdSense and related Google advertising technologies. Third-party ads are controlled by their providers and may use cookies or similar technologies according to their policies.

User responsibility

Visitors should review any ad destination before interacting with it. An advertisement appearing on the site does not mean Xiake Tools endorses the advertiser, product, or claim.

Ad-safe page selection

Not every public URL is a good place for advertising. Error pages, thin technical files, embedded game frames, or pages with controls that are easy to misclick should be excluded from ads. The preferred ad-supported pages are complete utility pages, policy pages, and navigation pages where visitors can understand the content before interacting.

Separation from content

Advertising should be visually separated from the main tool result, copy buttons, form fields, and navigation links. If an ad placement makes a page confusing or reduces trust, it should be removed or moved. The long-term goal is to keep free access available while protecting the usability of the tools.

Review and removal

If an ad placement creates confusion, appears too close to a control, slows down a tool, or makes the page feel less trustworthy, it should be reviewed and removed or moved. A stable free tool site depends on visitor trust more than on maximizing the number of ad placements on every page.

Policy consistency

The advertising policy should match the live site. If a future update adds new ad formats, the privacy policy and advertising policy should be reviewed together so visitors understand the role of cookies, third-party providers, and ad personalization controls.
