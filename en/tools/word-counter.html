Privacy and accuracy notes

Most calculations happen locally in your browser. Do not enter passwords you actively use, private student records, payment data, confidential documents, or sensitive personal information. Results are practical estimates for daily use and may differ from rules used by a school, publisher, platform, employer, or official authority.

Before you use the result

Check the result in the context where you plan to use it. A number, generated value, converted format, or random choice can be useful immediately, but the final decision still belongs to you. If the output will be used for school, publishing, business, health, finance, or a public activity, compare it with the rules of that platform or organization before relying on it.
