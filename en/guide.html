High-score guide

Do not get greedy

Short, safe holds are better than long risky holds. Release as soon as a warning appears.

Watch the rhythm

The teacher does not always turn at the same time. Treat every warning as real until you learn the current pattern.

Use sound cues

Audio helps you notice warnings faster, especially on mobile.
